As most Americans speak english all the words and phrases you would need to know on your trip are the same as home

Here are some common phrases you could use on your trip to fit in!

Spill the tea: Share the gossip
Go Dutch: People pay for their meals separately
Shoot the Breeze: Having a casual, pretty much meaningless conversation with someone
Behind the eight-ball: Finding oneself in a bad situation.
Gotta get flat: Gotta Lie Down
Plead the Fifth: They imply their refusal to answer any incriminating questions
Ballpark figure: A rough estimate on how much something costs
